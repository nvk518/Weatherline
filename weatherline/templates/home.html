Planning for your next vacation?


Whether it's a tour around Europe or a long-weekend roadtrip through the West Coast, use weatherline to plan for the weather throughout your journey.


What is a weatherline?


When you're visting a city for only two days, often times a 10 day forecast is unnecessary. A weatherline contains a one-day forecast for each day of your trip, so all the weather details you need are in one place, making packing for a vacation or business trip simpler and more effective.


How it works


1. Choose a start date for your trip.
2. Add a city and state for your first day and that day's forecast automatically fills in.
3. Repeat by adding cities for the rest of the days of your trip.
Only locations within the United States and Canada are supported at this time.
